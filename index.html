Design & Analysis of Algorithms – Course Overview

About the Course

This project is part of the Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA), Course Code 24ECSC205, taught by Prof. Prakash Hegade at KLE Technological University.

What the Course Is About

The course focuses on designing efficient algorithms, analyzing time and space complexity, and building optimal solutions for computational problems.

Structures & Algorithms Studied

  • Greedy Techniques
  • Dynamic Programming
  • Divide & Conquer
  • Backtracking
  • AVL Trees, Heaps, Hashing
  • Segment & Fenwick Trees
  • Skip Lists, Priority Queues
  • Dijkstra, Bellman–Ford, Floyd–Warshall
  • Sorting & Searching Techniques

Real-World Applications

  • Navigation – Shortest path algorithms
  • Social Media – Ranking algorithms
  • Databases – Indexing & hashing
  • Networking – Routing optimization
  • Compilers – Graph-based optimizations
  • AI & Robotics – State-space search
